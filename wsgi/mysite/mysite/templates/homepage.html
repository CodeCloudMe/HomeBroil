Home-cooked Food On-Demand

The open-source "Uber for Food"

This project was created to solve the problem of food waste. When you create too much of a great entree, upload it here, and let others near you buy it or pick it up for free. The vision of this project is to be a great way for people who cook at home to make additional income from their food and allow hungry people to get better food than a restaurant at better prices.

Currently, this service is only available in Seoul, Korea. However, if you want to add your city, please reach out to hello@homebroil.com
